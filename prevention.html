How to Prevent Diabetes

Preventing diabetes requires a combination of **lifestyle changes, healthy habits, and regular medical checkups**. Explore the best ways to stay healthy below:

1. Healthy Eating

Eating a balanced diet with **whole grains, lean proteins, and fiber-rich foods** helps maintain blood sugar levels.

2. Regular Exercise

Engage in at least **30 minutes of physical activity** daily to **increase insulin sensitivity**.

3. Maintain a Healthy Weight

Keeping a **healthy BMI** significantly reduces the risk of developing **Type 2 diabetes**.

4. Reduce Sugar & Processed Foods

Minimizing **sugary drinks and processed snacks** helps prevent insulin resistance.

5. Manage Stress

High stress can lead to hormonal imbalances and **higher blood sugar levels**.

6. Regular Health Checkups

Annual blood sugar tests help detect **early signs of diabetes** and take preventive action.
