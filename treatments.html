Managing and Treating Diabetes

Diabetes treatment focuses on managing blood sugar levels through various methods, including medication, lifestyle changes, and medical interventions.

1. Lifestyle Changes

Healthy eating and regular physical activity can help manage blood sugar levels and prevent complications.

2. Insulin Therapy

For people with Type 1 diabetes and some with Type 2, insulin injections or pumps are essential for managing blood sugar levels.

3. Oral Medications

Several types of oral drugs, like Metformin, help improve insulin function and reduce blood sugar levels.

4. Blood Sugar Monitoring

Regular blood glucose monitoring helps patients make informed decisions about their treatment.

5. Weight Management

Maintaining a healthy weight through diet and exercise can significantly improve diabetes control.

6. Advanced Medical Treatments

In some cases, pancreas transplants and artificial pancreas systems are considered for diabetes management.
